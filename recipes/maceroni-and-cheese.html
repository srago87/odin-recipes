Maceroni and Cheese


Home

Description

This receipe makes a cheesee maceroni and cheese with bread-crumbs

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups elbow maceroni
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup flower
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 block (16oz) velveeta cheese
  • pepper
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Mexican 4 style cheese blend
  • chedar cheese
  • bread crumbs
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups elbow maceroni to a boil and drain
  2. Melt whole stick of butter into the pot you used to boil the maceroni
  3. Set stove to medium-high heat, mix in 1/2 cup flour, stirring constatntly until you get a mixture with a consistancy similar to apple-sauce
  4. Mix in 1 cup of milk, stir until homogenous, mix in another cup of milk, stir constantly for 3 minutes
  5. Mix in 16 oz veleeta cheese, stir constantly for 5 minutes or until cheese is totally melted
  6. Mix in another cup of milk, similar to step 4
  7. Add some pepper to cheese-sauce
  8. pour boiled maceroni into baking dish and add cheese sauce
  9. Add desired amount of shredded mozzarella, shredded Mexican 4 style cheese blend, shredded cheddar
  10. Stir to mix the cheese with the noodles
  11. Add a layer of shredded cheese to the top of the dish
  12. Add layer of bread-crumbs on top of the shredded cheese layer
  13. Top it off with some parmesan
  14. Cover the dish, bake on 350 degrees for 10 minutes
  15. Remove dish from oven, remove cover
  16. Broil for up to 5 minutes to toast bread-crumbs

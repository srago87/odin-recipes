Chicken Tenders


Home

Description

This recipe makes simple, seasoned breaded chicken with few ingrediants.

Ingrediants

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 garlic cloaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 4 tbsp water
  • dried oregano
  • dried thyme leaves

Steps

  1. crack 2 eggs into a medium size bowl
  2. grind 4 garlic gloaves in with the eggs and mix
  3. add 1/2 tsp salt
  4. add 1/2 tsp pepper
  5. add 4 tbsp water and wisk
  6. pour bread-crumbs out on a plate
  7. add 1/2 tsp salt to bread-crumbs, along with 1/2 tsp pepper, dried oregano, and dried thyme leaves
  8. pour flour out on a seperate plate, add pinch of salt and pepper
  9. dip chicken tender in flour, then eggs, then seasoned bread-crumbs, cook in olive oil

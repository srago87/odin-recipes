Pulled Pork

Description

This recipe is for crock pot pulled pork. Requires few ingrediants and will take approximately 6-8 hours to prepare.

Ingrediants

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground mustard
  • 3 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 3 1/2 lbs boneless pork shoulder

Steps

  1. Using a sharp knife, remove the excess fat from the outside of the pork shoulder. Be careful not to remove all the fat from the meat.
  2. Add the brown sugar, garlic powder, gound mustard, salt, pepper, and smoked paprika to a mixing bowl. Stir until homogenous.
  3. Apply the dry rub to the meat, making sure to cover every crevise with the mixture.
  4. Add chicken stock and meat to the slow cooker. Cook on high for 4-6 hours or low for 8 hours.
